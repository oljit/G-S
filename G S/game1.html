RED DEAD REDEMPTION 2



Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world action-adventure video game developed and published by Rockstar Games.The game is a prequel to its predecessor Red Dead Redemption (2010) and the third in the Red Dead series. Its events take place in 1899, before the events of the first film, Red Dead Redemption, and follows outlaw Arthur Morgan, a member of Dutch Van der Linde's gang.
The game was released on PlayStation 4 and Xbox One on October 26, 2018, and on PC on November 5, 2019. Rockstar Games also updated Red Dead 2's discs and digital edition for the ninth generation PlayStation 5 consoles and Xbox Series X and S in 2020. The game received high praise from critics, who praised the story, open world, and amazing level of detail, with many calling it one of the best games of all time.The story of Red Dead Redemption takes place in the twentieth century, specifically in the year 1911 AD, and revolves around the character of John Marston, a former criminal who was working with a group of criminals who committed many crimes punishable by law, but they were always on the move, so the law could not arrest them, and after getting tired...
